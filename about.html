About me

I'm a product designer with a passion for including people in the margins of society in any project. I serve my teams as an active facilitator, communicator, critic, and coach. My favorite strengths are leadership, software, writing, and ethics. My background is in Art History, and I also happen to be a docent for the Architecture River Tours of Chicago. I love meeting and connecting people, so please: send me a note and tell me who you are, and how you found me!
